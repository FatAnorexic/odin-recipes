Lasagna Recipe

Homemade Lasagna

Continuing our series on food, this will help rid you of any excess cheese and sauce you may have left over from making pizza. Or perhaps, you were looking to simply make this as a standalone. Eitherway, lasagna is an amazingly delicious dish if not a little more involved than any of the other recipes in this list.

There are several key ingredients you'll need to start out with. I've listed them out below, but a short hand list would be:
cheese, noodles, seasonings, tomatoes,sweet italian sausage, ground beef, onion, garlic
Prep time usually takes an hour or two, with a final bake time of about 45 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Parmasean Cheese
  • 80/20 ground beef
  • Sweet Italian Sausage
  • Onion (sweet or spanish)
  • Two Cloves Garlic
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can tomatoe paste
  • Salt and Pepper
