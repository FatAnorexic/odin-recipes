Homemade Lasagna

Continuing our series on food, this will help rid you of any excess cheese and sauce you may have left over from making pizza. Or perhaps, you were looking to simply make this as a standalone. Eitherway, lasagna is an amazingly delicious dish if not a little more involved than any of the other recipes in this list.

There are several key ingredients you'll need to start out with. I've listed them out below, but a short hand list would be:
cheese, noodles, seasonings, tomatoes,sweet italian sausage, ground beef, onion, garlic
Prep time usually takes an hour or two, with a final bake time of about 45 minutes.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Ricotta Cheese
  • Mozzerella Cheese
  • Parmasean Cheese
  • 80/20 ground beef
  • Sweet Italian Sausage
  • Onion (sweet or spanish)
  • Two Cloves Garlic
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can tomatoe paste
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps:

  1. Start by bringing a 4quart pot of water to a boil. Add 2-3 table spoons course sea/kosher salt. water should be salty like the sea.
  2. Once water has begun boiling add lasagna noodles for about 8-9 minutes
  3. While noodles cook dice 2 cloves garlic and one onion finely.
  4. In a pan/cast iron skillet, begin browning italian sausage and ground beef on mid to mid-low heat. Season with salt, pepper and italian seasoning.
  5. Remove noodles when time is up and strain. Leave to the side for now.
  6. When meat is browned add onions and garlic and cook until they sweat on mid-low heat.
  7. Add canned tomatoes and dole paste in one teaspoon at a time until mixture thickens.
  8. In an oven safe baking cask, layer noodles, meat sauce, cheese (in that order) until half an inch from the top. Add last layer of noodles and cheese.
  9. Cover and bake at 375 F for about 45 minutes or until top cheese layer is browning.
  10. Let stand for 20 minutes before cutting into and enjoy.
